Medico is a secure mobile application that bridges patients to doctors to alleviate prolonged in-clinic wait times for non-emergency injury or illness

Features & Benefits

Security

Google authentication and Biometrics Login

Push Notification

Push notifications of new messages

Photo and Video

Chat feature with images and small videos sharing option

Flexibility

Flexibility to the schedule and location

Time Saving

Saving the time spent on traffic and waiting

Stay Safe

Reducing the number of patients that the doctors exposure to
